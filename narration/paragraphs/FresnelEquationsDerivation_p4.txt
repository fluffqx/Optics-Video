For s-polarisation: the tangential E is the full field (since E is already tangential). Continuity of tangential E gives E-i plus E-r equals E-t. Continuity of tangential H — using H equals n E over mu-naught c and the geometry — gives n-i times cosine theta-i times E-i minus E-r equals n-t times cosine theta-t times E-t. Solving these two equations gives the Fresnel coefficients r-s and t-s.
