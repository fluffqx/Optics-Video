Gauss's Law for the magnetic field states that the divergence of B is always zero — everywhere, without exception. This is a statement about the non-existence of magnetic monopoles. There is no magnetic equivalent of a charge — no isolated north or south pole. Every magnetic field line must form a closed loop. A field line that enters any closed surface must exit it. This is one of the great fundamental laws of nature — it has been tested to extraordinary precision and no violation has ever been found.
