Verify all four required properties of an electromagnetic wave from Bennett Section 2.3.1. First, E is transverse: E points in x-hat, which is perpendicular to the propagation direction z-hat. Second, B is transverse: B points in y-hat, also perpendicular to z-hat. Third, E is perpendicular to B: x-hat is perpendicular to y-hat. Fourth, E cross B points in the propagation direction: x-hat cross y-hat equals z-hat, which is the propagation direction z-hat. All four properties confirmed.
