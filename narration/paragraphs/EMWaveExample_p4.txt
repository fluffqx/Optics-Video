Now compute the Poynting vector: S equals one over mu-naught times E cross B. The cross product x-hat cross y-hat equals z-hat, confirming that energy flows in the z-direction — the propagation direction. The magnitude of S is E-naught squared divided by mu-naught c, which equals epsilon-naught c E-naught squared. Time-averaging over one cycle replaces E-naught squared with one half E-naught squared, giving the irradiance I equals one half epsilon-naught c E-naught squared.
