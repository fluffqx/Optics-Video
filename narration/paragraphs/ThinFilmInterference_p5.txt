Here is the systematic approach. List each reflection. For each one, ask: does n increase? If yes, add a pi phase shift to that beam. Compute the net phase difference between the two reflected beams: subtract the total phase shifts for beam 1 from those for beam 2. Add the geometric contribution 2 pi times 2 n-f t cosine theta-t over lambda-naught to get the total phase difference delta. Constructive reflection: delta equals 2m pi. Destructive reflection: delta equals 2m plus one times pi.
