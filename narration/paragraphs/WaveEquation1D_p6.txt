Bennett Section 1.3 gives the general solutions. Any function of the form f of x minus vt satisfies the wave equation, as does any function of g of x plus vt. This can be verified by substitution: computing partial squared f over partial x squared gives f-double-prime of x minus vt; computing partial squared f over partial t squared gives v-squared times f-double-prime of x minus vt; the ratio is exactly v-squared as required.
