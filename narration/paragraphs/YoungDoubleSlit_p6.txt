Complete numerical example: d equals 0.1 millimetres, L equals 1.5 metres, lambda equals 589 nanometres sodium yellow light. Fringe spacing equals 589 times ten to the minus 9 times 1.5 over 0.1 times ten to the minus 3 equals 8.84 millimetres. The fringes are about a centimetre apart — easily visible to the naked eye. The fifth bright fringe above centre is at y-five equals 5 times 8.84 equals 44.2 millimetres from the centre.
