A three-dimensional plane wave propagating in the direction of wave vector k-vector is psi-tilde of r-vector and t equals A e to the i k-vector dot r-vector minus omega t. The dot product k-vector dot r-vector selects the component of position along the propagation direction. Surfaces of constant phase — wavefronts — are the planes k-vector dot r-vector equals constant, which are flat planes perpendicular to k-vector.
