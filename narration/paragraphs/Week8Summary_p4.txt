The finesse from Bennett Section 7.10: script-F equals pi root R over 1 minus R. This equals the free spectral range divided by the FWHM of each transmission peak. Also: script-F equals pi root F over 2. For R equals 0.9: F equals 360, script-F equals 29.8. For R equals 0.99: F equals 39600, script-F equals 312.
