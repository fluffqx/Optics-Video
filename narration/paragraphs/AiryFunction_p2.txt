At resonance — when delta equals 2N pi for integer N — the sine term is zero and I-t equals I-naught. Complete transmission, regardless of R. Even if each mirror has 99 percent reflectivity, the cavity transmits 100 percent at resonance. The physical explanation: at resonance, all the multiply-reflected beams are in phase and interfere constructively in the transmission direction.
